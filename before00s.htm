Fighting games prior to the 2000s

During the 90s Fighting games had a huge spike in popularity, starting the year off strong with

This was the first iteration of Street Fighter 2 and DEFINITELY DEFINITELY not the last

Street Fighter 2 was a major improvement from the original Street Fighter and some versions are still played competitively today

in addition to Street Fighter 2 a new challenger approached

MORTAL KOMBAT!

with these two fighting game GIANTS on the table it was difficult for anyone else to contend with them, even with innovative design

But a new dimension came into the mix

Virtua fighter

The first fighting game to use 3d graphics in a fighter game, using polygons instead of sprites to make its characters 3 dimensional

After these games the most important fighting games were usually sequels to other games

Including:

  • Street fighter 2 Champion edition
  • Street fighter 2 Turbo
  • Street fighter 2 Hyper fighting
  • Street fighter 3
  • Street fighter 3: 2nd impact
  • Street fighter EX plus
  • Street fighter EX2
  • Street fighter EX2 plus
  • Street fighter 3: 3rd strike
  • Mortal Kombat 2
  • Mortal Kombat 3
  • Mortal Kombat Trilogy
  • Mortal kombat Mythologies: Sub-Zero
  • Mortal Kombat 4

Needless to say the 90s were a huge time for fighting games, and heavily influenced the future of fighting games
