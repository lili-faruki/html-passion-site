Fighting games during the 2000s

Fighting games during the 2000s may not have been as influential as fighting games during the 90s, however many fighting games during this era were still important to the growth of certain franchises

While the original Super Smash Bros came out during 1999, Super Smash Bros Melee is still played to this day and overall was much more influential to the series as a whole
