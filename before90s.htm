Fighting games prior to the 90s

The first fighting game ever made was Heavyweight Champ and released in Japan, being the third highest grossing arcad video game of 1976

But it was Karate Champ that fully popularized the one on one fighting video game style

In the game the player could choose from twelve different locations around the world and fight a "best out of 3" competition to win, being the first large fighting game it sold extremely well and was a "Commercial blockbuster" in arcades and recieved the "Gold award" from the SPA for over 100,000 sales
